Starters:

- Prawn Cocktail

	Ingredients:	 
	400g cooked Atlantic Shell-on Prawn
	4 Little Gem Lettuce, washed and trimmed
	5 Heaped tbsp mayonnaise
	5 tbsp tomato chutney
	2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
	2 tsp creamed horseradish
	Tiny splash Tabasco sauce
	Squeeze lemon juice
	Paprika, for dusting
	4 tsp snipped chive
	
Peel all but 4 of the prawns (reserve these for the tops).
Break the lettuces into individual leaves, then divide the leaves evenly between 4 small glass bowls.
Sprinkle the prawns over the lettuce and season with black pepper.
Mix the mayonnaise, tomato chutney, Worcestershire sauce, horseradish and Tabasco together. 
Season to taste with lemon juice and salt and pepper, then spoon sparingly over the prawns. 
Dust the top with a little paprika and sprinkle with chives. 
Top with the remaining prawns and serve immediately. 
Delicious with brown bread.

Bruschetta

Ingredients:

5 tomatoes
5 onions
5 tbsp olive oil
pinch of Italian seasoning
3 loaves of bread

Recipe:
Cut tomatoes, onions and put in a bowl, add the olive oil and some Italian seasoning, mix well. Then, toast the bread, spread bruschetta on bread and serve.

Mushroom Soup

	Ingredients:

		- 1 pound mushrooms
		- 1 lemon
		- 1 tsp butter
		- 2 tsp minced shallots
		- 0.25 tsp thyme
		- 0.5 tsp bay leaf
		- 1 tsp salt
		- 0.5 tsp ground pepper
		- 2 cups heavy cream
		- 1.5 cups chicken stock
		- 1 tsp cornstarch
		- 1 tsp parsley

	Directions:

		-1)	Sprinkle the mushrooms with lemon juice.
		-2)	In a food processor, coarsely chop them.
		-3)	Melt the butter in a heavy saucepan and lightly saute the shallots.
		-4)	Add the mushrooms, thyme, and bay leaf and saute over moderate heat for 10 minutes, or until the liquid disappears.
		-5)	Add the salt, pepper, cream, and chicken stock and bring to a boil.
		-6)	Reduce the heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
		-7)	Add the cornstarch to the soup and continue to simmer 10 minutes longer, stirring constantly. Correct seasoning to taste.
		-8)	Serve in warm bowls. Sprinkle with minced parsley.

Tuna Dip

Ingredients: 
- 170g Tuna
- 1 tsp Mayonaisse
- 1 tsp Peppered Mustard
- 85g Yoghurt
- 1 tsp  Lemon Juice
- 2 tsps Pickles.

Step 1: Add the following ingredients tuna, mayonnaise, mustard, yoghurt and lemon juice, into a medium bowl. 
Step 2: Mix the bowl.
Step 3: Blend the mixture until smooth. 
Step 4: Stir in the pickles.
Step 5: Keep chilled until serving.
