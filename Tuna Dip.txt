Tuna Dip

Ingredients: 
- 170g Tuna
- 1 tsp Mayonaisse
- 1 tsp Peppered Mustard
- 85g Yoghurt
- 1 tsp  Lemon Juice
- 2 tsps Pickles.

Step 1: Add the following ingredients tuna, mayonnaise, mustard, yoghurt and lemon juice, into a medium bowl. 
Step 2: Mix the bowl.
Step 3: Blend the mixture until smooth. 
Step 4: Stir in the pickles.
Step 5: Keep chilled until serving.
