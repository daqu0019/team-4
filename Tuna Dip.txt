Tuna Dip

Ingredients: 
- 170g tuna
- 1 tsp mayonaisse
- 1 tsp peppered mustard
- 85g yoghurt
- 1 tsp  lemon juice
- 2 tsps pickles.

Step 1: Add the following ingredients tuna, mayonnaise, mustard, yogurt and lemon juice, into a medium bowl. 
Step 2: Mix the bowl.
Step 3: Blend the mixture until smooth. 
Step 4: Stir in the pickles.
Step 5: Keep chilled until serving.
