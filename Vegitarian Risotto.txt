Vegetarian Risotto

Ingredients: 
- Olive oil
- 200g risotto rice
- 700ml vegetable stock
- 200g pesto
- 100g soft goat's cheese


Step 1: Pour a glug of olive oil into a large saucepan. 
Step 2: Tip in the rice and fry for 1 minute. 
Step 3: Add half the stock and cook until absorbed. 
Step 4: Add the remaining stock, a ladle at a time.
Step 5: Cook until the rice is al dente, stirring continually, for 20-25 mins.
Steo 6: Stir through the pesto and half the goat’s cheese. 
Step 7: Serve topped with the remaining cheese.
