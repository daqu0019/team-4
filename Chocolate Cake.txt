Chocolate Cake

Ingredients: 
- 250g  white sugar
- 225g all purpose flour
- 100g cocoa powder
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp salt
- 150ml milk
- 75ml vegetable oil
- 2 eggs
- 150 ml boiling water
- 2 tsp vanilla extract	

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). 
Step 2: Grease and flour two nine inch round pans.
Step 3: In a large bowl, stir together the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda and salt. 
Step 4: Add the eggs, milk, oil and vanilla and mix for 2 minutes on medium speed of mixer. 
Step 5: Stir in the boiling water last. 
Step 6: Pour evenly into the prepared pans.
Step 7: Bake 30 to 35 minutes in the preheated oven. 
Step 8: Cool in the pans for 10 minutes.
Step 9: remove to a wire rack to cool completely.
