Mushroom Soup

	Ingredients:

		- 1 pound mushrooms
		- 1 lemon
		- 1 tsp butter
		- 2 tsp minced shallots
		- 0.25 tsp thyme
		- 0.5 tsp bay leaf
		- 1 tsp salt
		- 0.5 tsp ground pepper
		- 2 cups heavy cream
		- 1.5 cups chicken stock
		- 1 tsp cornstarch
		- 1 tsp parsley

	Directions:

		-1)	Sprinkle the mushrooms with lemon juice.
		-2)	In a food processor, coarsely chop them.
		-3)	Melt the butter in a heavy saucepan and lightly saute the shallots.
		-4)	Add the mushrooms, thyme, and bay leaf and saute over moderate heat for 10 minutes, or until the liquid disappears.
		-5)	Add the salt, pepper, cream, and chicken stock and bring to a boil.
		-6)	Reduce the heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
		-7)	Add the cornstarch to the soup and continue to simmer 10 minutes longer, stirring constantly. Correct seasoning to taste.
		-8)	Serve in warm bowls. Sprinkle with minced parsley.
